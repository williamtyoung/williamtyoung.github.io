A Brief Summary
Education & Academics
I am a data scientist in the US Navy with a master's degree in computer science from the University of Virginia, and a bachelor's degree in Cyber Operations from the U.S. Naval Academy. My research interests include cyber policy, data science, machine learning, and the Internet of Things (IoT). I am proud to work with amazingly dedicated and talented people on a wide range of cutting-edge problems.

Athletics
I spent 15 years of my life as a competitive swimmer (100/200 breaststroke, 400 IM) and was fortunate enough to attend the US Junior National Championships five separate times. I have won individual championship titles at both the state and national levels. In 2012, I qualified to compete for a position on the US Olympic Team in two events at the US Olympic Team Trials in Omaha, Nebraska. I enjoyed a brief stint on the world top 100 list in the summer of 2011 and (as of 2016) my name could still be found on the all-time top 100 lists for multiple age categories.

Interests
I am a big fan of self-improvement books, strategy, hiking, music, and a hot cup of coffee.
